Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who are we?

Jong Foodie is a personal food blog run by Merdan Koc. The website is accessible at www.jongfoodie.com.

2. What data do we collect?

We collect the following data:

  • Email addresses of newsletter subscribers (via Mailchimp)
  • User account data (name, email) for logged-in users (via Firebase)
  • Anonymous visitor statistics

3. What do we use cookies for?

We use cookies for:

  • Functionaliteit: Keeping track of whether you are logged in
  • Analytics: Anonymously tracking visitor numbers
  • Nieuwsbrief: Mailchimp tracking to see if emails are opened

4. Your rights (GDPR)

As an EU resident you have the right to:

  • View, correct or delete your data
  • Withdraw your consent for cookies
  • Object to the processing of your data

Send an email to info@jongfoodie.com for questions about your data.

5. Refusing or deleting cookies

You can decline cookies via the cookie banner at the bottom of the page. You can also use your browser settings to delete or block cookies.

6. Changes

This policy may be updated. The most recent version will always be available on this page.
