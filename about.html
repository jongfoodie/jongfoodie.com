From Rotterdam to the world

I'm Merdan Koc, born and raised in the Netherlands, but my heart belongs to food. For as long as I can remember I've been fascinated by restaurants — not just the food, but the atmosphere, the story behind a dish, the people who make it.

Jong Foodie started as a personal project: I wanted to keep track of which places are truly worth visiting and which aren't. No fake reviews, no sponsored content. Just honest opinions.

I've now visited over 52 restaurants across 6 countries — from the Netherlands to Turkey, the USA, Thailand, the UAE and Aruba. Every place tells a story, and I share that with you.

My mission

Honest food, honest reviews

The food world is full of sponsored content and fake reviews. Restaurants pay for good scores, influencers get free meals in exchange for positive posts. That's not how I work.

I always pay for my own food. I visit restaurants anonymously. And I tell you exactly what I think — even when it disappoints. Because you deserve honest information before spending your evening (and money) somewhere.
