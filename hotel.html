Hotel The Craftsmen

★★★★★
📍 Singel 83, 1012 VE Amsterdam 💰 Boutique

A stunning boutique hotel right on the Singel canal in the heart of Amsterdam. The craftsmanship in every detail is remarkable — from the hand-picked furniture to the bespoke interiors. A truly unique stay that captures the soul of Amsterdam.

Must Visit Canal View Boutique Amsterdam
🌴

Petit Ermitage

★★★★★
📍 8822 Cynthia St, West Hollywood, CA 💰 Luxury

A hidden gem in West Hollywood — part boutique hotel, part art gallery. The rooftop pool and bar attract a creative crowd and the vibe is unlike anything else in LA. Intimate, artistic and effortlessly cool. One of those places you never want to leave.

Must Visit Rooftop Pool Art Gallery West Hollywood
🛕

The Peninsula Bangkok

★★★★★
📍 333 Charoen Nakhon Rd, Bangkok, Thailand 💰 Luxury

Arguably the finest hotel in Bangkok. Perched on the banks of the Chao Phraya River, the views are simply spectacular. The service is flawless — every staff member anticipates your needs before you even ask. The infinity pool overlooking the river at sunset is a memory that stays with you forever.

Must Visit River Views World Class Service Bangkok
